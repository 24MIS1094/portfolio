To use real app icons for WhatsApp, GitHub, LinkedIn, and Instagram:

1. Download the following icons:
   - WhatsApp: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/6b/WhatsApp.svg
   - GitHub: https://github.githubassets.com/images/modules/logos_page/GitHub-Mark.png
   - LinkedIn: https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/simple-icons/simple-icons/icons/linkedin.svg
   - Instagram: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/e/e7/Instagram_logo_2016.svg

2. Convert SVGs to PNGs if needed (use https://www.svgviewer.dev/ or any online converter).

3. Rename the files:
   - whatsapp.png
   - github.png
   - linkedin.png
   - instagram.png

4. Place them in this images folder.

5. Refresh your portfolio site to see the icons.
